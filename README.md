> Microservices-based hospital management platform built with Spring Boot

## 🏥 Overview

MediCore is a modern, cloud-native hospital management system built using microservices architecture with Spring Boot and Spring Cloud. The platform provides comprehensive solutions for patient management, appointment scheduling, medical records, pharmacy operations, billing, and real-time notifications.

### Key Highlights

- 🏗️ Microservices Architecture - Independently deployable services for scalability
- ⚡ Reactive Programming - WebFlux for high-concurrency notification service
- 🔄 Event-Driven - Asynchronous communication via Kafka
- 🔐 Secure - JWT authentication with Keycloak
- 📊 Observable - Distributed tracing, metrics, and monitoring
- 🐳 Cloud-Ready - Containerized with Docker and Kubernetes support
- 🧪 Test-Driven - Comprehensive unit, integration, and E2E tests

## ✨ Features

### 👤 Patient Management
- ✅ Patient registration with complete demographics
- ✅ Search by name, DNI, or insurance number
- ✅ Manage allergies and pre-existing conditions
- ✅ View appointment history
- ✅ Update contact information

### 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Management
- ✅ Doctor profiles with specialties and certifications
- ✅ Configurable weekly schedules
- ✅ Block specific dates (vacations, conferences)
- ✅ View daily agenda
- ✅ Search doctors by specialty

### 📅 Appointment System
- ✅ Real-time availability checking
- ✅ Book, cancel, and reschedule appointments
- ✅ Automatic email and SMS confirmations
- ✅ 24-hour reminders
- ✅ Appointment history tracking

### 📋 Medical Records
- ✅ Digital medical history
- ✅ Diagnoses and treatment plans
- ✅ Electronic prescriptions
- ✅ Document attachments (X-rays, lab results)
- ✅ Complete patient timeline


### 💊 Pharmacy
- ✅ Prescription management
- ✅ Inventory control
- ✅ Medication dispensing
- ✅ Low stock alerts
- ✅ Batch and expiration tracking

### 💰 Billing
- ✅ Automatic invoice generation
- ✅ Insurance integration
- ✅ Payment processing
- ✅ Patient statements
- ✅ Financial reports

### 📧 Notifications (Reactive)
- ✅ Multi-channel notifications (Email, SMS, Push)
- ✅ Event-driven processing
- ✅ Notification history
- ✅ Template management
- ✅ Automatic retries

### 📊 Monitoring & Observability
- ✅ Distributed tracing with Zipkin
- ✅ Metrics collection with Prometheus
- ✅ Custom dashboards in Grafana
- ✅ Circuit breaker patterns
- ✅ Centralized logging
